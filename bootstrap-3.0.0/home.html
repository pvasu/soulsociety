About

Formed in 2010, Soul Society has played at the hottest venues in San Francisco, including Grant & Green, 50 Mason Social House, and Neck of the Woods. They are also available for weddings, birthday parties, and other private occasions.

Armed with 2 singers and a horn section on top of drums, bass, guitar, and keys, they are a talented crew that loves to play soul, R&B, and funk - especially upbeat tunes that get crowds moving!
